Lasagna Recipe

Indulge in the layers of deliciousness with this homemade lasagna recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Add chopped onions and garlic, and cook until onions are softened.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water, oregano, basil, salt, and black pepper to the skillet. Simmer the sauce for about 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  4. In a greased baking dish, layer the lasagna as follows: a third of the meat sauce, a layer of lasagna noodles, a third of the mozzarella cheese, a third of the Parmesan cheese, and half of the ricotta cheese. Repeat the layers, ending with the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  5. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Let it cool for a few minutes, slice, and serve.
