Risotto Recipe

Enjoy the creamy goodness of homemade risotto with this simple recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth, heated
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pan, saut√© chopped onions and minced garlic in olive oil until softened.
  2. Add Arborio rice to the pan and stir to coat with oil, allowing the rice to toast slightly.
  3. Pour in the white wine and cook until it is mostly absorbed by the rice.
  4. Begin adding the heated broth one ladle at a time, stirring frequently and allowing the liquid to be absorbed before adding more.
  5. Continue this process until the rice is creamy and cooked to al dente texture.
  6. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese, and season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  7. Remove from heat, let it rest for a few minutes, and serve warm.
