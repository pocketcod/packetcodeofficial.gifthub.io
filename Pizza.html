Pizza Recipe

Try making this delicious homemade pizza:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Dried oregano and red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce and add your favorite toppings.
  6. Drizzle olive oil over the pizza and sprinkle with dried oregano and red pepper flakes if desired.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven, let it cool slightly, slice, and enjoy!
