Pasta Recipe

Here is a delicious pasta recipe for you to try:

Ingredients:

  • 250g of pasta
  • 2 cups of tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato sauce to the pan and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Combine the cooked pasta with the sauce.
  6. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
